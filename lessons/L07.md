# LESSON 07: "WHAT WILL HAPPEN NEXT?"
**Grammar:** Future Questions | **Theme:** Process Explanation

## VOCABULARY (8 words)
happen, proceed, continue, advance, notify, inform, decide, announce

**Audio:** "Explaining the Recruitment Process" - Answering candidate questions: "What will happen next? First, we'll review all applications. Then we'll contact selected candidates. After that, we'll schedule interviews..."

**Grammar:** When/Where/How + will + subject + base verb? | Will + subject + base verb?

**Typical Candidate Questions:**
- When will you contact me?
- How long will the process take?
- Will you call or email?
- What will the interview include?
