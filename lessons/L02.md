### 4. A DIFFICULT RECRUITMENT WEEK

**Audio Script:**

"Let me tell you about a challenging week I had last month.

On Monday, I posted a new job opening for an HR Coordinator position. I expected maybe 30 or 40 applications because the position required three years of experience. By Tuesday afternoon, I received 150 applications! I didn't expect so many people to apply.

I started screening CVs on Wednesday. Unfortunately, many candidates didn't qualify for the position. They didn't have the required experience or they didn't have the necessary education. About 100 applications didn't meet our minimum requirements.

On Thursday, I sent interview invitations to 15 qualified candidates. But here's the problem: five candidates didn't respond to my email. I waited two days, but they didn't answer. Maybe they didn't see the email, or maybe they accepted other offers.

On Friday, I scheduled interviews for the following week. I sent calendar invitations to 10 candidates. However, on Monday, two candidates didn't attend their interviews. They didn't call, they didn't email, they just didn't show up. I was disappointed.

In the end, I didn't hire anyone that week. The process took three more weeks. Sometimes recruitment doesn't go as planned, but that's the reality of our work."

### 5. UNDERSTANDING NEGATIVE FORMS

**Grammar Discovery:**

Look at these sentences:
- "I **didn't expect** so many applications."
- "They **didn't qualify** for the position."
- "The candidate **didn't respond**."

**Questions:**
- What word appears in all negatives? (didn't)
- What form is the main verb? (base form, no -ed)
- When do we use didn't? (past negative)

---

### 6. PAST SIMPLE NEGATIVE: THE RULE

**Grammar Table:**

| Subject | didn't | Base Verb | Example |
|---------|--------|-----------|---------|
| I | didn't | work | I didn't work yesterday. |
| You | didn't | apply | You didn't apply on time. |
| He/She/It | didn't | qualify | She didn't qualify. |
| We | didn't | expect | We didn't expect this. |
| They | didn't | attend | They didn't attend. |

**Key Rules:**

1. **Formation:** didn't + BASE VERB (never -ed)
   - ✓ I didn't work
   - ❌ I didn't worked

2. **All Subjects Same:** No changes for he/she/it
   - ✓ He didn't work
   - ✓ She didn't work
   - ✓ It didn't work

3. **Full Form:** did not = didn't
   - "I did not expect" (formal)
   - "I didn't expect" (common)

**Spanish (A2):** Para negativo, usa didn't + verbo base (sin -ed).
