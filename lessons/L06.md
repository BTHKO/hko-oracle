# LESSON 06: "I'LL SEND YOU THE JOB DESCRIPTION"
**Grammar:** Future (will) - Offers/Promises | **Theme:** Professional Commitments

## VOCABULARY (8 words)
send, forward, share, call, email, provide, explain, confirm

**Audio:** "Making Commitments to Candidates" - Series of promises: "I'll send you the job description today. I'll call you tomorrow. I'll forward your CV to the manager. I'll let you know by Friday..."

**Grammar Table:**
```
WILL + BASE VERB
I'll/You'll/He'll/She'll/We'll/They'll send
Questions: Will you...? | Will she...?
Negative: won't (will not)
```

**Use Cases:** 
- Offers: "I'll help you" 
- Promises: "I'll call you tomorrow"
- Instant decisions: "The phone is ringing. I'll answer it."
