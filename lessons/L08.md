# LESSON 08: "THIS CANDIDATE IS BETTER THAN THAT ONE"
**Grammar:** Comparatives | **Theme:** Candidate Evaluation

## VOCABULARY (8 words)
experience, qualification, skill, background, knowledge, ability, performance, potential

**Audio:** "Comparing Three Candidates" - Manager discussion: "Maria has more experience than Carlos. Carlos is younger than Maria but he's more qualified. Roberto is the strongest in technical skills..."

**Grammar Table:**
```
SHORT ADJECTIVES: -er + than
strong → stronger | old → older | young → younger

LONG ADJECTIVES: more + adj + than
experienced → more experienced
qualified → more qualified

IRREGULAR:
good → better | bad → worse
```

**Comparison Structures:**
- Maria is MORE experienced THAN Carlos
- This CV is BETTER THAN that one
- She has LESS experience THAN him
