# LESSON 05: "I'M GOING TO REVIEW THE CVS TOMORROW"
**Grammar:** Future (going to) - Plans | **Theme:** Recruitment Planning

## VOCABULARY (8 words)
review, schedule, contact, shortlist, arrange, prepare, send, follow-up

**Audio:** "Tomorrow's Recruitment Tasks" - Recruiter planning: "Tomorrow I'm going to review 30 CVs. I'm going to shortlist 10 candidates. I'm going to contact them by email. I'm going to schedule interviews for next week..."

**Grammar Table:**
```
BE + GOING TO + BASE VERB
I'm/You're/He's/She's/We're/They're going to review
Questions: Are you going to...? | Is she going to...?
Negative: I'm not going to... | He isn't going to...
```

**Contrast:** GOING TO (plans) vs WILL (decisions/offers) - introduced here, detailed in L6
