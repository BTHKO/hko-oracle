# LESSON 09: "SHE'S THE MOST QUALIFIED CANDIDATE"
**Grammar:** Superlatives | **Theme:** Final Selection

## VOCABULARY (8 words)
best, strongest, top, leading, outstanding, excellent, superior, ideal

**Audio:** "Final Selection Meeting" - "After reviewing all candidates, Maria is the most qualified. She has the strongest background. She's the best fit for our team. She has the most relevant experience..."

**Grammar Table:**
```
SHORT: the -est
strong → the strongest | old → the oldest

LONG: the most + adjective
qualified → the most qualified
experienced → the most experienced

IRREGULAR:
good → the best | bad → the worst
```

**HR Contexts:**
- The best candidate for the position
- The most experienced recruiter
- The strongest interview performance
- The highest qualifications
