# LESSON 11: "HAVE YOU WORKED HERE LONG?"
**Grammar:** Present Perfect Basic | **Theme:** Experience & Duration

## VOCABULARY (8 words)
worked, been, had, managed, handled, led, coordinated, supervised

**Audio:** "Discussing Tenure" - Office conversation: "I've worked here for five years. I've been in HR since 2019. I've managed recruitment for three years. I've hired over 50 people in this company..."

**Grammar Table:**
```
HAVE/HAS + PAST PARTICIPLE
I/You/We/They have worked
He/She/It has worked

Questions: Have you worked...? | Has she worked...?
Negative: haven't/hasn't worked
```

**Time Expressions:**
- FOR: for five years, for two months (duration)
- SINCE: since 2019, since January (starting point)

**Use:** Connect past to present (still relevant now)
- I've worked here for 3 years (and I still work here)
- vs. I worked there for 3 years (past job, finished)
