Write the Final Message

Grammar ends in guided writing: short professional email or message.

ES: Escribe un correo o mensaje breve usando la estructura.

Writing mission

  • Write a short email about your normal work day.
  • Use at least 4 correct present simple sentences.
  • Add a greeting and closing.

Template notes built into the shell

  • Keep later stages more cognitive: vocabulary stage 6 = words/phrases, stage 7 = recorded speaking; grammar stage 6 = full sentences, stage 7 = guided writing.
  • Every free production task includes a suggested-answer dropdown.
  • Every stage can carry Back, Next, Save, Retry, Check as needed.
  • Interactive sets marked randomize-group should shuffle items on load in the final functional version.
  • Recording blocks are placeholders for Moodle-compatible recording and feedback tools.
