📧 Useful Language · Stage 7

Write your email

Situation: You finished a recruitment process last week. Write to your manager, Maria, to summarise what happened. Include: how many applications you received, what the shortlist looked like, and how the interview went.
💡 Suggested Answer
Hi Maria,

I wanted to update you on the recruitment process for the HR Coordinator role.

We received 45 applications last week. I reviewed them carefully and created a shortlist of six candidates. The interviews went very well — three candidates performed really strongly. I didn't expect such a high quality of applicants.

I'll send you the final shortlist today.

Best regards,
Paula

📄 Lesson Transcript

The full text from the dialogue used in this lesson.

Maria: Hi Paula. How did the recruitment process go?

Paula: It went really well. We received over 80 applications in the first week. I didn't expect so many.

Maria: That's impressive. Did you find any strong candidates?

Paula: Yes, I shortlisted six people. The interviews were last Thursday. Three candidates stood out immediately.

Maria: How long have you worked in HR, Paula?

Paula: I started five years ago. I worked in a small team at first, then moved into a senior recruiter role two years later.
